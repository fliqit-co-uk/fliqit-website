Privacy policy

Last updated: 18 July 2026

What we collect

Fliq.it collects the information needed to provide the app: account details, profile information, plans and messages you create, profile photos, flow history, and preferences. With your permission, we may access location, notifications, calendar, reminders, or contacts to provide the related feature.

How we use information

We use information to operate and improve Fliq.it, personalise plans, enable social features, provide support, send requested communications, and keep the service safe.

Sharing

Plans, profile details, and messages you choose to share can be visible to the relevant participants. We do not sell personal information. We use service providers to operate the app, including infrastructure, authentication, and communications providers.

Your choices

You can update profile and communication preferences in the app, withdraw optional device permissions through your device settings, and delete your account from Privacy & Safety. For help, email hello@fliqit.co.uk.

Contact

For privacy questions or requests, contact hello@fliqit.co.uk.
