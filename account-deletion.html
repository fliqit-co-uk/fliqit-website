Delete your Fliq.it account

You can delete your account directly in the Fliq.it app.

How to delete an account

Open the app, go to Profile → Privacy & Safety → Close Account, and follow the confirmation steps. You will be signed out when deletion completes.

What is deleted

We delete your account, authentication record, profile, preferences, personal calendar flows, friend requests, notifications, messages, reviews, and account-linked data.

Shared plan history

If other people participated in a plan or wave you created, the shared activity may remain so their flow history is preserved. Your identifying details are removed and replaced with “Deleted user”; your membership and personal contributions are removed.

Need help?

If you cannot access the app, email hello@fliqit.co.uk from the email address associated with your account. We may ask for information needed to verify the request.
