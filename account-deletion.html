A SIMPLE PATH

How to delete your account

01

Open your profile

Open Fliq.it and head to your Profile.

02

Find Privacy & Safety

Open Privacy & Safety, then choose Close Account.

03

Confirm deletion

Follow the confirmation steps. You will be signed out when it completes.

THE ESSENTIALS

What is deleted

We remove the personal data connected to your Fliq.it account, including:

✦

Your account

Authentication record, profile, preferences, and account-linked data.

◌

Your activity

Personal calendar flows, friend requests, notifications, messages, and reviews.

↗

Your presence

Your plan memberships, pending requests, and profile references held by friends.

◎

A NOTE ON SHARED MOMENTS

History can stay. Your identity does not.

If other people participated in a plan or wave you created, that shared activity may remain so their flow history is preserved. Your identifying details are removed and replaced with “Deleted user”; your membership and personal contributions are removed.
